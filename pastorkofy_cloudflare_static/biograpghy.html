Pastor Kofy Biography

Updated Mar 5, 2026

Pastor Kofy (Francis Blewusi-Kofi Dedzi) is widely known in Ghana as a gospel musician, worship minister, and event host whose public identity centers on praise-led gatherings, worship releases, and community impact initiatives.

Profile Overview

  • Known for energetic live ministry and worship-focused stage leadership.
  • Public coverage links his growth to the Mentor-era visibility period.
  • Frequently associated with Royalhouse-oriented worship environments and major event stages.
  • Brand direction combines music ministry, public events, and social impact messaging.

Ministry Focus

  • Youth outreach messaging through school-facing ministry activity.
  • Community-facing efforts including philanthropy and peace advocacy initiatives.

Public Timeline Highlights
